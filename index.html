The Connection Card

MEET FIVE PEOPLE.
DO IT WITH PURPOSE.

Every attendee receives a Connection Card at check-in. Five specific prompts so you know exactly who to find and what to say. Complete all five during the networking hour and earn your first Fundable Passport points.

Your Connection Card · Five Prompts

Complete each one during the networking hour from 4:00 to 5:00 PM and during the structured connection moments throughout the evening. All five earn points on your Fundable Passport.

1
Find a CRA officer or funder representative. Ask them one question about what they fund right now.
2
Find someone who serves a different neighborhood than yours. Exchange names and org information.
3
Find someone whose mission could complement yours. Could you partner on a grant application together?
4
Find someone who has never applied for a grant. Tell them the one thing they need to know first.
5
Find someone from a different cultural or faith community. Learn what they are working on tonight.
